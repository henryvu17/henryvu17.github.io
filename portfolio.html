Description

The story behind this game was for my partner and I to make a game that resembles the 1942 Arcade Game but, a 2D type of side scroller.
The start of the game shows megaman trying to fight of robot enemies due to a robot, alien, and underworld invasion. Scoring was on the count of hits.
Random selection occurs as the enemy spawns. The game gets harder as time goes by. The user loses when they hit 0 HP never ending, intended to win by hit points.

Reflection

The point of making this game was influenced by the Arcade Game 1942. The Most successful part of the game was the difficulty over time.
If more time was given, the general ending and continuation of the story would be improved and gameplay.
